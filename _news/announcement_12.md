🎉 Our work **STEPH** (Sparse Task Vector Mixup with Hypernetworks for Efficient Knowledge Transfer in Whole-Slide Image Prognosis) is accepted to [CVPR 2026](https://cvpr.thecvf.com/Conferences/2026). As a continuation of [CROPKT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.13482), this work presents an efficient solution: *prognosis knowledge transfer via model merging*. Please see our [paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10526) and [code](https://github.com/liupei101/STEPH) for more details.
